An efficient, organized system for activity documentation — turning a 20-minute manual chore into a 2-minute guided conversation in Telegram.

Overview

Documenting activities manually takes up far more time than most people realize: opening Drive to create folders, uploading photos one by one, reading and summarizing documents by hand, filling in Sheets row by row, then sending a confirmation email. Add it up and it's 15–20 minutes per activity — over 3 hours a month for just 10 entries.

This system replaces that entire process with one short conversation in Telegram. Answer a few guided questions, send your photos and documents, and within seconds everything is stored in Drive, summarized by AI, logged to Sheets, and confirmed by email — automatically.

How It Works
1
Type "hi" in Telegram The bot guides the user through the data entry step by step — Date, Activity Code, Activity Name, PIC.
2
Send photos and documents Activity photos, attendance photos, and PDF/Docs are received and uploaded automatically to a dedicated Google Drive subfolder.
3
AI summarizes the documents PDF documents are analyzed by Gemini and summarized in 3–5 sentences, focused on decisions and follow-up actions.
4
Logged to Google Sheets The complete record — including the Drive folder link and documentation status checkmarks — is logged automatically.
5
Confirmation email sent An email lands in the inbox with all the details of the entry just saved — useful as an audit trail.
