About Me

From data analysis
to intelligent automation

I started my career as a Data Analyst — spending 3+ years asking "what does this data mean?" through SQL queries, Python scripts, and business dashboards.

That analytical foundation led me to a bigger question: "What should happen next — automatically?" I began integrating AI and workflow automation into my work, building systems that don't just surface insights, but act on them in real time.

Today I sit at the intersection of data, AI, and automation — using n8n, Claude, and APIs to build end-to-end intelligent workflows. I'm currently in transition, still working with data while actively expanding into full automation engineering.
