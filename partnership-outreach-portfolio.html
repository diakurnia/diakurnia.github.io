Partnership Outreach Automation

An end-to-end email outreach system built entirely on Google Workspace — from opportunity discovery through AI email generation, human approval, automated follow-ups, and reply tracking.

Type: Workflow Automation
Platform: Google Apps Script
Status: Production Ready
Google Apps Script Claude AI Gmail API Google Sheets
Overview

Most partnership outreach fails not from lack of effort, but from inconsistency — emails sent too late, follow-ups forgotten, and no visibility into who responded. This system eliminates those gaps by automating the entire outreach lifecycle inside Google Sheets, requiring no external infrastructure or paid subscriptions.

Built for organizations managing multiple funding calls and partner profiles simultaneously, the system monitors incoming opportunities, matches them to the right partners, generates tailored emails via the Claude API, routes them through a human approval step, and then handles all sending, tracking, and follow-up sequences automatically.

How It Works
1
Opportunity Ingestion A scraper deposits new funding calls and tender opportunities into the "Incoming" tab. Duplicates are detected and filtered automatically.
2
AI Email Generation Valid opportunities are matched to partner profiles. Claude API generates a personalised outreach email for each match.
3
Human Approval Draft emails appear in a review sheet. A team member checks a checkbox to approve before any email is sent.
4
Sending & Tracking Approved emails are sent via Gmail API. Thread IDs are logged for reply monitoring. Contact cooldown (30 days) is enforced.
5
Follow-up Sequences No reply after 7 days triggers a first follow-up. A second follow-up fires at day 14. Rows auto-close after no response to the second follow-up.
Key Features
  • AI-generated personalised emails via Claude API
  • Human-in-the-loop approval before sending
  • Automated day 7 and day 14 follow-ups
  • Reply detection and thread tracking
  • 30-day contact cooldown enforcement
  • Do Not Contact list enforcement
  • Duplicate opportunity detection
  • Full error logging with row references
  • Zero external infrastructure required

Architecture Note
Zero infrastructure cost. The entire system runs on Google Workspace — Apps Script as the backend, Sheets as the database, Gmail as the delivery layer. No servers, no cloud functions, no additional paid services. The human approval step is intentional: AI generates, humans decide, automation executes.
